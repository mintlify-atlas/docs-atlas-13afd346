## Community Guidelines

### What to Expect

The NapCat community is welcoming and helpful, but please note:

**This is a non-profit project.** For integration issues, basic questions, or underlying framework issues, please search for solutions on your own — the community does not provide such support.

### Best Practices

When asking for help:

1. **Search first** - Check if your question has been answered
2. **Be specific** - Describe your issue clearly with details
3. **Include context**:
   - NapCat version
   - NTQQ version
   - Operating system
   - Relevant configuration (remove sensitive tokens!)
   - Error messages
   - Steps to reproduce
4. **Be patient** - Community members volunteer their time
5. **Be respectful** - Follow the code of conduct
6. **Give back** - Help others when you can!

### What Not to Ask

The community cannot help with:
- Basic programming questions ("How do I use Node.js?")
- General integration tutorials ("How do I use Nonebot2?")
- Third-party application issues
- Questions already answered in documentation

For these topics, please search online or consult the relevant project's documentation.

### Community Rules

1. **Be respectful** - Treat everyone with kindness and respect
2. **Stay on topic** - Keep discussions relevant to NapCat
3. **No spam** - Don't post advertisements or off-topic content
4. **No harassment** - Zero tolerance for harassment of any kind
5. **Follow the law** - Comply with local laws and regulations
6. **Don't mention in other communities** - Do not mention NapCat in other protocol-side or related application-side project communities to avoid disputes

### Code of Conduct

NapCat follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct.html).

**Reporting Violations**

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to community leaders at nanaeonn@outlook.com.
